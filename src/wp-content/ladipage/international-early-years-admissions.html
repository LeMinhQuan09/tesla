TESLA EDUCATION - IB WORLD SCHOOL

Tesla Education offers the International Baccalaureate (IB) Integrated Program from Early Years (EYP) to Primary Years (PYP) and Middle Years (MYP) exclusively in Tan Binh District (Ho Chi Minh City).

International Early Years

For children from 18 months to 4 years old

The Early Years Program at Tesla fosters children's cognitive, emotional, and social skills development.

TESLA EARLY YEARS PROGRAM

Chương trình giảng dạy
  • For children under 3 years old, the program follows a project-based integrated approach with the Ministry of Education and Training’s curriculum. Children will explore, experiment, and learn through their own experiences.
  • For children over 3 years old, they will follow two programs: the International Program and the Approach Program of the International Baccalaureate Primary Years Program (IB PYP), which supports the development of thinking, cognition, emotions, and social skills.

The Early Years Program is
implemented through 6 key areas

  • Practical life skills education fosters children's coordination, concentration, independence, and sense of order.
  • Inquiry-based learning through play creates a healthy and positive learning environment, supporting children’s comprehensive development.​
  • Meaningful play activities allow children to learn at their own pace and according to their needs.
  • Children will develop their English skills with native teachers.
  • Phonics helps children familiarize themselves with listening, identifying, and using different sounds to distinguish words.
  • It lays the foundation for language skills, supporting their learning in both English and other subjects.
